Appointy

Multi-Vendor SaaS

"Appointy" is a full-featured multi-vendor SaaS booking platform developed using Laravel, Vue.js, Stripe, and REST APIs. The system allows administrators to manage subscription plans and platform settings, vendors to create service businesses and manage bookings, and customers to discover vendors and schedule appointments online. Key features include subscription billing, free trials, staff management, availability scheduling, appointment booking, payment processing, invoicing, vendor dashboards, and role-based access control. The application is designed to scale across multiple service industries through a single SaaS platform.
